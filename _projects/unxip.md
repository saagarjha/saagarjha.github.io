title: "unxip"
date: 2022-02-04
description: >
  Fast Xcode unarchiver designed as a validation test for <a href="https://docs.swift.org/swift-book/documentation/the-swift-programming-language/concurrency/">Swift Concurrency</a>. A series of careful design descisions allow it to be used for streaming decompression, as a embeddable library, or on platforms such as Linux. Performance-wise it beats Apple's implementation by about 2x in speed and a smidge when it comes to on-disk size of the final result.
